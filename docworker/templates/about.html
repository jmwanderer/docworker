About DocWorker

DocWorker uses GPT AI models to summarize, analyze, and transform docouments.

ChatGPT and other GPT AI language models deliver amazing functionality by taking text as input and generate text as output. These models can chat with humans, write an email, and answer questions. Howver, GPT AI language models are are limited in the amount of text that can be processed in a single operation. If we want to feed the GPT a large document, the document needs to be broken up into sections and each section processed individually.

This is what DocWorker does. DocWorker accepts PDF, DOCX, and TXT documents, breaks them into sections, feeds individual sections to the GPT AI model, and combines the results back into one document.

For example, a person may want to extract the key concepts from the Supreme Court decision XXX which is published as a PDF file.
